Education & Experience

My name is Immanuela, and I am the founder of Owate Consulting. Owate Consulting was established with a clear mission to address the severe misinformation that often plagues young professionals in the labor market. We aim to empower them, helping them overcome early career disadvantages and achieve long-term career stability.

In my career journey, I discovered the transformative power of personal branding, targeted job hunting, networking, and creative storytelling. These innate skills helped me to advance seamlessly in my professional life. Thus, I decided to assist professionals in navigating their career journeys effectively. The term "young professionals" is often associated with students or recent graduates. However, within this context, it encompasses individuals new to the workforce, regardless of age or educational status.
